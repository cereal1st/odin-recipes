Chilli Con Carne

Description

Chilli con carne is a hearty and delicious dish that's perfect for a cozy dinner. Here's a recipe that serves 4-6 people:

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • A pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: chopped jalapeno peppers, shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  3. Drain any excess fat from the pot, then add the kidney beans, diced tomatoes, chilli powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Bring the chilli to a simmer and cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened and the flavours have melded together.
  5. Taste the chilli and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  6. Serve hot, with any optional garnishes you like.
