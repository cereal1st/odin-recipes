Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Pasta arrabiata is a classic Italian dish that's packed with flavor and heat, thanks to the combination of garlic, tomatoes, and red pepper flakes. Here's a recipe that serves 4-6 people:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 28-ounce can of whole peeled tomatoes, crushed by hand
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
  • Optional: Fresh basil leaves, torn

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the penne pasta and cook until al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes and cook until the garlic is fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and salt to the skillet and stir well to combine. Simmer the sauce for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the skillet with the arrabiata sauce. Toss well to coat the pasta with the sauce.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves (if using). Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
